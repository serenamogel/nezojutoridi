Fabiana Actress

Fabiana has appeared in a number of movies and television shows. Her film roles include portraying the daughter of a World War II Resistance fighter in The Scarlet and the Black co-starring Gregory Peck, Christopher Plummer, and John Gielgud. On television, she had the role of Giulietta on the ABC soap opera One Life to Live from 1985 to 1986 and was a regular cast member in the syndicated drama Amazon written by Peter Benchley.

Fabiana has guest starred and had recurring roles on a number of television shows, including NYPD Blue, Babylon 5, Baywatch, Full House, Mad About You, Wings, The Magnificent Seven, CSI: Miami, Mistresses, and Quantum Leap. She was cast also in the recurring role of Elena Di Nola/Mutter in the series Jane the Virgin
