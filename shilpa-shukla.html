Shilpa Shukla is an Indian star who has been working as a film and theatre actress and has given marvelous performances. She is notable for her roles in the sports Bollywood blockbuster film named ‘Chak De! India’ which was released in 2007.

She was born on 22nd Feb 1982 in Bihar, India. She comes from a Bhumihar family. Her forefathers were associated with professions, like; bureaucrats, political leaders, and scholars.

Career

Shilpa Shukla chose acting as a full-time career. She is notable for her role in ‘Chak De! India’ in which she starred alongside the Bollywood superstar Shahrukh Khan and many others who played significant roles.

She started her career with a film named ‘Khamosh Pani’ which was a French-German Collabroation. The actress. then didn’t stop working as an actress and continued acting.

Shilpa Shukla then appeared in ‘Hazaron Khuwahishein Aisi’ which was released in 2005. In the film, she appeared as Mala.

Later, she became famous for her movies, like; ‘B.A Pass’ for which she won the Filmfare Critics Award for the category of Best Actress.

Achievements

In 2007, Shilpa Shukla became a part of a most popular film named ‘B.A Pass’ for which she was awarded the Filmfare Critics Award for Best Actress.
