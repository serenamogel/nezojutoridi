Rocky Road Pudding Pops

These Rocky Road Pudding Pops are the perfect treat for a summer afternoon!

Course Dessert, Snack Cuisine American Keyword pudding pops, rocky road pudding pops Prep Time 10 minutes minutes Freeze 4 hours hours Total Time 4 hours hours 10 minutes minutes Servings 6 popsicles Calories 258kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 2 cups TruMoo low fat chocolate milk
  • 3.9 oz chocolate pudding mix 1 box
  • 1/2 cup miniature chocolate chips
  • 3/4 cup miniature marshmallows
  • 1/4 cup roughly chopped almonds

Instructions

  • Combine chocolate milk and pudding mix and whisk for two minutes.

  • Stir in remaining ingredients and spoon into popsicle mold.

  • Insert popsicle sticks and freeze for at least four hours.

  • To remove popsicles, run under warm water for just a few seconds until the pops loosen up enough to easily pull out.
