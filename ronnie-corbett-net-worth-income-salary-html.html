‘Ronnie’ Corbett (born 4 December 1930) is a Scottish actor and comedian best known for his work with Ronnie Barker in the television comedy series The Two Ronnies. He was awarded the CBE for services to entertainment. He first came to public attention as a guest on Sir David Frost’s satirical comic show The Frost Report in the 1960s, and he subsequently appeared in the comedies Sorry! and No – That’s Me Over Here!
