Sathish Krishnan is the most-talented person who flaunts his skills. He is an actor, choreographer, and dancer, hailing from India and worked in Tamil cinema. He never fails to win the hearts of his fans with a dashing and charismatic personality.

Career

Sathish Krishnan has worked in Tamil cinema and started his career as an actor.  He is the instructor of Flyerz Dance Company where he worked for over 10 years. Apart from this, he made his career on television as well. He has acted in various serials, such as; “Aattam Paatam”, “Dance Machi Dance” and “Maanada Mayilada”. Moreover, he was honored with the title of Best Dancer, Jodi No1 Season-4, and many dance reality TV shows as well.

Apart from this, he made his first movie as a choreographer in “Neethane En Ponvasantham” and also worked in various hit movies, such as; “Yennai Arindhaal” “Vaalu”, “Madras” and others.

In 2007, he appeared in a movie named “Unnale Unnale”. Later, he got an opportunity to play the lead role in Gautham Menon’s directorial “Chennaiyil Oru Mazhaikaalam”. Also, he appeared in the movie “Mudivilla Punnagai” which was directed by Srikanth. While other movies “Uppu Karuvadu” and “Endrendrum” as well.

In 2008, he worked in the movie “Vaaranam Aayiram” in which he played a small role. Also, he choreographed all songs of a movie named “Achcham Yenbadhu Madamaiyada.”

However, he worked in both films and television serials and marked his identity in the Tamil cinema to win the hearts of many.

Achievements

Among various prestigious awards and honors, he earned a nomination of the Filmfare Award for Best Supporting Actor-Tamil.
