Julia Fox Sliced off Her Waistband and Wore It as a Bra Top

March 04, 2024

If there's one thing Julia Fox knows how to do, it's make headlines — whether it's regarding her love life or her scantily clad outings. After all, during her stint with Kanye West, the actress practically single-handedly sponsored the comeback of ultra low-cut pants.

And on Thursday, Fox was spotted bringing the trend to her jeans. Except it appears that they didn't initially come with that cut. Because the waistband was removed from her pants and ... worn as a bra top. She paired the light-wash, ab-baring denim look with matching knee-high, pointy-toed boots and a tote bag made out of jeans with the legs tied together to create a strap.

This wasn't the first time that the actress made a case for a sexy Canadian tuxedo. Back when Kanye was courting her, the couple wore matching denim ensembles, which included a cropped jacket with swirled cone bra cups for Julia.

The tabloid-beloved couple officially called things off last month, and her team confirmed the split with a statement that read, "Julia and Kanye remain good friends and collaborators but they are no longer together."

Julia quickly moved on to become someone else's "muse," walking in the LaQuan Smith runway show at New York Fashion Week. Smith told WWD that it was an easy decision for Julia to make her catwalk debut with his brand, saying, "Julia has been so supportive of me from my early days. She always comes to me for a nasty catsuit for a night out on the town and it just felt kind of pressy and gaggy and I was like, 'let's just do it.'"
