Yuka Terasaki

Yuka Terasaki (Japanese: 寺崎裕香 Terasaki Yuka) is a Japanese voice actress for the Pokémon anime.

Biography

Terasaki was born in Kumamoto Prefecture, Japan on August 4, 1983, and became a voice actress and singer as an adult. Terasaki began to provide voices for the Pokémon anime near the end of Pokémon the Series: Black & White. She has since provided the voices of a few characters of the day. During Pokémon the Series: XY, she voiced Korrina, Ash's Fletchinder, Ash's Noibat, and Clemont's Luxio, her most prominent roles until Pokémon Horizons: The Series, where she voiced a Main Character for the first time, in the form of Roy. Terasaki is currently affiliated with Ogipro the Next.
