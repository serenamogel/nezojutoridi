Hasan Minhaj (born September 23, 1985) is an American Comedian, Actor, Political Commenter, Television host, Producer, TV Personality and Writer. He has appeared in many Famous Stand-up Comedy Shows, Movies and Television series.

In addition, he has also acted in his debut film “True Stories from My Crappy Childhood” in 2010. Later, he has got his lucky break to be selected for his “Hasan Minhaj: Homecoming King” original movie by Netflix. This Stand-up Comedy Film has released on 23 May 2017 worldwide on Netflix.
